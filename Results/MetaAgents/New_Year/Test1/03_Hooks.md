## 6. Channel-Readiness Check

- **Placement/Format Fit**  
  - All hooks designed for Reels/TikTok/Shorts (9:16, 15–25s).  
  - First 1–2 seconds contain bold overlay hooks readable at small size and with sound off.  
  - Safe margins respected: titles and CTAs within central 80% of frame.

- **Policy & Claims**  
  - Avoid words like “cure,” “treat disease,” or references to serious conditions (cancer, etc.). Use “support,” “help with everyday issues,” “what I use in my family.”  
  - Emphasize personal/use-case storytelling and educational framing (“here’s how this book organizes herbal options”) rather than guaranteed outcomes.

- **Measurement & Testing Plan (3x3)**  
  - **Top 3 hooks to test:**  
    - Hook 1 (midnight fever / clinic closed) – high emotional spike.  
    - Hook 7 (pharmacy receipts + $1 remedy) – strong budget tension.

  - **3 opening visual variants each:**  
    1. Direct scene (as described).  
    2. Caption-only cold open on solid background for 1s, then scene.  
    3. Immediate close-up of book page before zooming out to situation.
