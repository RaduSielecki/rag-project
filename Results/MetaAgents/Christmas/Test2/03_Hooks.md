## 6) Channel-Readiness Check

- **Placements/Formats**  
  - TikTok, IG Reels, FB/IG in‑feed, Stories: 9–20s duration; vertical 9:16; safe margins for captions and overlays.  
  - Use burnt‑in captions + platform-native captions for accessibility.  
  - Thumbnails: clear frame of the book or the high‑tension moment (blackout, messy drawer, nightstand).

- **Policy/Compliance**  
  - Phrase as “natural support,” “home remedies families have used,” “helps you make your own herbal preparations” rather than claiming to cure, treat, or prevent diseases.  
  - Include brief disclaimers where needed: “Not a substitute for professional medical care; see your doctor for serious symptoms.”

- **Measurement & Testing (3x3 Creative Test)**  

  - **Matrix:**  
    - Top 3 hooks (by conceptual strength across segments):
