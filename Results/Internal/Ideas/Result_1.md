1. “Symptom Roulette” (Curiosity + Remedy-Oriented)  
Ad concept: Creator spins a DIY “symptom wheel” (parasites, hair loss, herpes, anxiety, joint pain, high blood pressure). Wherever it lands, they MUST flip to that remedy in The Forgotten Home Apothecary and show it in 3 seconds.  
Hook examples:  
- “Let’s play symptom roulette with this banned-looking book…”  
- “Whatever this wheel lands on, I’ll show you the remedy from my home apothecary. No cuts.”  
Why it stops the scroll: Fast, game-like, highly visual; viewers wait to see “their” problem pop up and then see there’s a remedy waiting inside the book.
